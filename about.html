About GlobalTrendUp

We make the world's trending topics accessible, understandable, and useful — in real time.

What We Do

GlobalTrendUp is a real-time trend intelligence platform that aggregates and analyzes trending topics from across the globe. We collect data from major search engines, social platforms, and news sources to give you a clear picture of what the world is talking about right now.

Whether you're a content creator, marketer, researcher, or simply curious about the world, GlobalTrendUp helps you stay ahead of the curve with AI-powered analysis and weekly, monthly, and yearly trend reports.

Why We Built This

Trend data is everywhere — but it's fragmented, hard to compare across countries, and rarely explained in context. We built GlobalTrendUp to solve that problem: one place to see what's trending in South Korea, the United States, Japan, and beyond, with meaningful analysis that helps you understand the "why" behind the numbers.

Our Mission

We believe that understanding what people care about — across cultures and languages — leads to better decisions, better content, and better conversations. Our mission is to make global trend intelligence accessible to everyone, not just large enterprises with big data budgets.

GlobalTrendUp launched in 2026 and is currently in Beta. We're actively improving the platform based on user feedback and expanding our country coverage every month.
